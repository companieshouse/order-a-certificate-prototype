Order a certificate

Use this service to order a signed certificate of incorporation for a company that includes good standing information.

Certificates can be delivered to a UK address or collected from a Companies House office.

Ordering a certificate takes around 5 minutes.

Fee

Certificates cost:

  • £50 for express delivery or collection (next day, or same day for collections ordered before 2pm)
  • £15 for standard delivery or collection (within 4 working days)

You can pay by debit or credit card. You cannot pay using a Companies House account.
